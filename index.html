We actively engage Adolescents and young Persons to help them achieve positive health outcomes.We are currently implementing our first project ARVs O'Clock - We visit Adolescents and Young Persons Living with HIV and support them through their journey to viral supression. We offer Health talks, counseling services and engage them in field activities to boost their morale and offer them wrist watches to help in keeping theirtime of taking ARVs. This goes a long way in helping them adhere to their medication.

Services also include:

  1. Wall Clock distribution for vulnerable families.
  2. Referrals and Linkage for medical conditions.
  3. Household supplies for vulnerable families.
  4. HIV/AIDs health talks and facilitation for caregivers.
